Water Use & Heating


  • Be mindful of wasted water. Running sinks, long showers, leaky hoses, etc.

    • Americans use incredible amounts of water when compared to people from some other parts of the world. We should be grateful and use this blessing wisely.

  • Know where your water cut-off is, and use it occasionally to ensure that it is in proper working condition.
