Energy Efficiency:
Saving Money and Resources

There are many ways to save energy while in a dwelling. We all use so many devices and machines in a day to use huge amounts of energy. Being concious of the energy we are using allows us to be more efficient in our daily lives.

These pages are dedicated to ways to save energy in one's home. Most are simple fixes which anyone could implement personally. Hopefully some will, and save money and help ease the environmental strain caused by our energy usage.
