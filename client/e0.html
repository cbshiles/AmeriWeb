Energy Efficiency:
Saving Money and Resources

There are many ways to save energy within one's home. With all the devices we operate and various parts of the house that consume energy, it is easy to be wasteful without realizing it. Being conscious of the energy we are using allows us to be more efficient in our daily lives.

These pages are dedicated to ways of saving energy from a homeowner's perspective. Most are simple fixes which anyone could implement personally. Hopefully it will encourage some will, saving money and helping to ease the environmental strain caused by our energy usage.
